Introduction

Welcome to the Mannheim Master in Management Analytics - Accounting Class of 2024 practical application sessions. Over the next two days, we will delve into the application of R and Excel to address prevalent business challenges, with a particular focus on web-scraping. Our aim is to equip you with insights into the automation and optimization of routine workflows.

While the sessions are designed in R, a foundational understanding of programming is crucial. We will methodically break down the advanced techniques used in the code for better comprehension.

Resources and Assistance

Engage in guided in-class exercises throughout these sessions. This year, we’re bolstered by the support of Large Language Models (LLMs). I strongly advocate for the utilization of these tools to amplify your learning experience. Here’s a snapshot of some leading LLMs:

  • ChatGPT (3.5/4.0): A distinguished tool in the AI domain.

  • Perplexity AI: Harnessing the GPT-4 framework, it offers native web-searching capabilities.

  • Google Bard: A notable AI tool in the market.

  • Claude AI: Accessible primarily in US/UK; VPN can provide broader access.

For effective prompting, consider:

  • PromptPerfect: Enhance your prompting skills.

  • PromptoMania: An AI-driven prompt generator.

Disclaimer:

LLMs may produce diverse results. Some might provide direct solutions, while others may necessitate further adjustments. The primary goal is understanding, not necessarily mastering every coding detail. The overarching principle is: If it’s effective, it’s right!
